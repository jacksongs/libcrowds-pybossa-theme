What's this all about?

Two journalists from the Canberra Press Gallery want to fix the Interests Register for good. BURN THE REGISTER (all caps please) will crowd-source the digitisation of the old paper and PDF entries to make them searchable, then lobby for freshly clarified, real-time, categorised, standardised, digital declarations. All this to allow long-suffering Parliamentary stuff to BURN THE REGISTER, and for politicians to win back the trust of the community.
